Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

An easy and quick sandwich classic. Mixing two great main ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Take ingredients and lay out.
  2. Spread peanut butter with knife on one piece of bread.
  3. Spread grape jelly with knife on opposite piece of bread.
  4. Stick the two slices together with peanut butter and jelly sides.
  5. Serve for one!
