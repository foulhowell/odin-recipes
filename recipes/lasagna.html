Lasagna

Description

An Italian dish of layers of cheese and meat. Baked to perfection. Loved by cats who also hate mondays.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Meat
  • Orange Cat (not optional)

Steps

  1. Layer cheese and meat on top of each other in glass oven-safe pan.
  2. Set oven to 425 degrees.
  3. Place pan in oven and let cook for 1 hour.
  4. Pull out pan and let cool for 15 minutes.
  5. Feed dish to cat!
